Itching to stretch my web-redesign muscles, I was on the hunt for a website that not only needed a visual update, but was also actually relevant to me and my peers. After asking around, my friend led me to the USA Computing Olympiad website -- he had participated in one of their contests when he was younger but the outdated design of their site always stuck with him.
